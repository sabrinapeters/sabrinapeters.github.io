Librarian, Researcher, Avid Reader & Wife

I'm a library assistant, researcher, and MLIS student at the University of Washington (incoming as of Fall 2014).

Since I work in a public library currently, my main responsibility can be easily described as educating those who are computer illiterate, as well as furthering technological literacy in general, with e-readers and tablets becoming more common.

While I love empowering individuals with the freedom that knowledge brings, my first love has always been research. Curiosity sparks into a flame of questions that will not be quenched until the answers are found.
